Instructions for Downloading and Installing draw.io:
	1. Open this link:
		https://www.drawio.com/
		click on "Download" button
		In the page opened, reach out the section:
			Assets
			Under Assets, click on the link "draw.io-21.2.8-windows-installer.exe link
			Once the installer.exe is downloaded to your computer:
				go to your Downloads folder and double click the .exe file
				Now it should launch the installer
				Follow the on-screen questions and answer:
					yes for the first question (seeking your permission to install)
					accepting the installation location that is highlighed
					Now click the install button
		This should complete the installation.
		
	2. In case you have run into any issue, we can fix that in the next session.
